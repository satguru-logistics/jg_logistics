About Us

A cold-chain logistics partner built around one job: holding temperature.

JG Logistics is a specialised refrigerated transportation and cold-chain logistics company providing reliable temperature-controlled solutions across India. Since 2024, we've focused on the safe, timely movement of temperature-sensitive goods through a modern reefer fleet and a dedicated logistics network.

Whether it's pharmaceuticals, dairy products, frozen foods, fresh produce or healthcare supplies, JG Logistics ensures uninterrupted cold-chain delivery from pickup to destination — with the quality, compliance and operational discipline the cargo demands.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient and technology-driven refrigerated transportation that preserves product quality and strengthens supply chain performance for our clients.

Our Vision

To become one of India's most trusted cold-chain logistics partners by delivering excellence in temperature-controlled transportation.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What temperature range can your reefer trucks maintain?

Our fleet maintains chilled transport from +2°C to +8°C and frozen transport from −18°C to −25°C, with multi-temperature vehicles available for mixed loads.

Do you provide pharmaceutical transportation?

Yes. We offer GDP-compliant pharmaceutical transportation for medicines, vaccines and healthcare products.

Is GPS tracking available?

Every vehicle in our fleet is GPS-enabled with real-time tracking and continuous temperature monitoring.

Do you offer Pan India transportation?

Yes, we operate across major states and metropolitan cities throughout India from our New Delhi base.

Can I get a dedicated reefer vehicle?

Yes, dedicated fleet solutions are available for businesses with recurring or contract transportation needs.

How quickly can a vehicle be arranged?

Our on-demand reefer services are built for fast deployment — contact our team for current availability in your route.

Do you provide temperature reports?

Yes, digital temperature recording and reports are provided for every shipment, supporting compliance documentation.

Are your services available 24/7?

Yes, our operations and support team are available around the clock.
